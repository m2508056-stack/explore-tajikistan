Hisor

Ancient Fortress of Tajikistan

The Historical Treasure

Hisor is one of the oldest historical cities in Tajikistan, famous for the legendary Hisor Fortress and its rich cultural heritage.

Hisor Fortress

The ancient Hisor Fortress is one of Tajikistan’s most iconic landmarks, attracting thousands of tourists every year.

Historical Importance

Hisor was an important center on the Silk Road and has a history stretching back more than 2,500 years.

Natural Beauty

The city is surrounded by green valleys, mountains, and beautiful landscapes perfect for tourism and photography.

Modern Development

Today, Hisor combines ancient architecture with modern infrastructure, becoming one of the main tourist destinations near Dushanbe.

City Facts

  • Population: ~30,000
  • Region: Districts of Republican Subordination
  • Known for: Hisor Fortress
  • History: 2500+ years
  • Near: Dushanbe
