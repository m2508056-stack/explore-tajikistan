Nurek

City of the Legendary Dam

The Hydropower Capital

Nurek is one of Tajikistan’s most famous industrial cities, known worldwide for the gigantic Nurek Dam and breathtaking mountain landscapes surrounding the reservoir.

Nurek Dam

The Nurek Dam is among the tallest dams on Earth and plays a key role in supplying electricity to Tajikistan.

Natural Beauty

The city is surrounded by mountains, turquoise waters, and beautiful panoramic views that attract tourists and photographers.

Economic Importance

Nurek is an important center of hydropower production and engineering development in Central Asia.

Modern Development

Modern roads, energy infrastructure, and tourism projects continue transforming Nurek into one of Tajikistan’s most important cities.

City Facts

  • Population: ~30,000
  • Region: Khatlon
  • River: Vakhsh
  • Industry: Hydropower
  • Known for: Nurek Dam
