Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to visit Tajikistan?

Summer (June-August) for mountain trekking, or spring/autumn for milder weather and festivals.

Do I need a visa?

Citizens of many countries can get visa-free entry for up to 90 days. Check current regulations.

Is Tajikistan safe for tourists?

Yes, Tajikistan is generally safe for tourists. Follow standard travel precautions and respect local customs.
